About Me

I am a recent CSUSM History graduate and arts administrator with hands-on experience at Escondido History Center, turning historical
materials into engaging public stories. I specialize in digital archiving, short-form content creation, exhibition support, and making complex
history accessible to everyday audiences. My work focuses on creative storytelling that connects people to place and culture, exactly the case
on my investigation on the early development of Escondido, CA, and on the historicity of Our Lady of Mount Carmel parish at Rancho Peñasquitos.
