Escondido History Center

The Thomas Brothers' Report

Analyzed, oranized, and contextualized
dozens of historical letters and
photographs dating from the 1890s to the
1960s into a structured public history
report. The project transformed archival
materials into an accessible narrative
explaining the Thomas Brothers' role in the
early development of Escondido, tracing
their migration from Wisconsin, business
activities, and community connections
within Southern California.

The Thomas Brothers' Report: The Thomas and Beven Heir's Letter

Organized 1960s family letters and
photographs into a clear chronological
public report for museum visitors and
digital archives. Transformed raw historical
documents into an engaging story of
Escondido's early development.
